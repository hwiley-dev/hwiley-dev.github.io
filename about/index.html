About

Accessibility-minded creative and technical work.

Hunter Wiley works across accessibility, music technology, and operational systems where clear interfaces and reliable workflows matter. His work centers on building and communicating tools that are easier to use, easier to understand, and more effective in real conditions.

Current areas of focus include accessible music production workflows, human-centered product design, and technical systems work shaped by field robotics and infrastructure operations. The goal of this site is to document that mix of creative practice and practical engineering with enough structure to stay useful over time.
