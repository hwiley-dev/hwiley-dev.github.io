Roots

Music + technology + mindfulness.

I grew up between recording studios and outdoor spaces. Today, I bridge those influences by crafting soundtracks, interactive environments, and tools that help people slow down. My practice blends Ableton, SuperCollider, modular synths, spatial audio, and modern web/mobile stacks.

Whether I am designing generative patches, building a tactile controller, or mixing a sound bath album, the goal stays the same: create a safe and inspiring container for listeners.

Values

Every project is guided by listening, experimentation, and care.

Listening first

Before touching a synth or script, I gather stories from facilitators, audiences, and stakeholders. The emotional blueprint drives the technical plan.

Prototype brave ideas
